This is a deck of cards with Christmas movies on it. That's it. It's not a game, it's not a "thing". It' just a buncha cards and each one has a Christmas movie on it.

You can get the deck by making it yourself. All the print-ready cards are on Github. Why would you get them? I don't know, if you don't get it, you don't get it.

I typed out some of the list by hand, and used ChatGPT to round out the rest. Once I had my movie list, I used ChatGPT to gather the ratings, years, etc.

I created a simple Google Docs spreadsheet with all my "data" inside and then set out to make a set of cards.

I used Figma to make a base card component, then used Contento to insert all my spreadsheet data into the card component array. Then I saved all generated cards as PNGs. Voila.

And the website? I had ChatGPT write my list of img tags, and used Tailwind for a copy/paste layout (including the scroll snap swiper thing). It even wrote the JS for this FAQs expanded thing. Neat!
